TACTICAL WATERS

Hot/cold radar · Moving fleets · 5 attack types

How to play
  • Each turn: attack once, then optionally move one ship by 1 cell.
  • Hot/Cold: bombs that miss reveal proximity — Burning / Warm / Cold / Freezing.
  • Sonar Ping: no damage, returns distance tier + compass direction to nearest ship.
  • Torpedo: fires across a row or column, hits the first ship in its path.
  • Recon Plane: reveals a 3×3 grid as occupied/empty (no damage).
  • Missile Barrage: 3-cell line damage, no info.
  • Bombed zones: any cell that was bombed can't have a ship moved into it for 4 of your turns.
  • Cooldowns: Sonar every 2 own turns, Torpedo every 3, Recon every 4, Barrage every 5.
